The drone advantage

A safer, faster, smarter way to clean — at any height.

01

Zero work-at-height risk

Operators stay grounded. No ladders, no MEWPs, no fall arrest — eliminating the leading cause of construction-sector fatalities.

02

Up to 60% faster than scaffolding

A four-storey commercial facade typically takes a single day, versus 7–10 with traditional access. No road closures. No tenant disruption.

03

HD inspection footage included

Every clean comes with 4K aerial documentation — a verifiable, time-stamped condition report you can hand straight to insurers or tenants.

04

Pure-water, eco-safe chemistry

Reverse-osmosis pure water leaves no residue and requires no detergents on most surfaces. Biodegradable softwash where chemistry is needed.
