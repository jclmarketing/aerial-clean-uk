A complete aerial cleaning service.

Six core specialisms, one operator. Whatever your building's wearing — algae, atmospheric grime, soiling on solar, lichen on render, traffic film on signage — we'll restore it without lifting an inch off the ground.

01 — Window cleaning

Crystal-clear glass on the buildings nobody else can reach.

High-rise office blocks, glass curtain walls, atriums, listed buildings, conservatories with vaulted apexes — our drones reach surfaces that would take a week of scaffolding in a single morning. The pure-water reach-and-wash system on board lifts dirt without detergents, dries spot-free and won't damage glass coatings.

  • Reach up to 70m vertical without access plant
  • 0 ppm pure water — no streaking, no spotting
  • Safe on solar control films, ceramic coatings and self-cleaning glass
  • Recurring service contracts available — quarterly, monthly, bespoke
02 — Solar panel cleaning

Restore the output you paid for.

Pollen, bird fouling, agricultural dust, urban PM2.5 — soiling can wipe up to 30% off panel output. Our soft-brush and pure-water systems are calibrated specifically for PV: no abrasion to the anti-reflective coating, no harsh chemistry, no risk to your warranty. Pre- and post-clean output benchmarking on request.

  • MCS / manufacturer-warranty compliant methodology
  • Suitable for ground-mount, rooftop and barn-conversion arrays
  • Thermal imaging upgrade — spot dead cells and hotspots in one visit
  • Output report comparing pre/post kWh delivered post-clean
03 — Gutter cleaning & inspection

See exactly what's in your guttering — before we clear it.

Most gutter contractors guess. We don't have to. A pre-clean drone survey gives you 4K HD walk-through of every linear metre, with annotated stills showing blockages, sagging, joint failures and overflow points. Then we clear what needs clearing and document the finished state.

  • Survey-first approach — quote based on evidence, not assumption
  • Vacuum-assisted clearance from ground level
  • Full digital condition report delivered same day
  • Insurance-ready documentation for managing agents & landlords
04 — Facade & cladding

Soft-wash chemistry, surgical accuracy.

Sandstone, limestone, brick, terracotta, ACM panels, ceramic, anodised aluminium — every substrate has a different chemistry. We pre-test, dwell, agitate and rinse with calibrated pressure to lift carbon, biological growth and atmospheric grime without damage. Heritage and listed buildings welcome.

  • Substrate-specific chemistry — no one-size-fits-all
  • Test patches before any large-scale application
  • Listed building experience — Historic England aware methodology
  • Biocide treatment optional — long-term regrowth prevention
05 — Render & K-Rend

Bring crisp, white render back from the green.

K-Rend, monocouche and silicone render are magnets for algae, lichen and atmospheric staining. We use TX10-style biocide soft-wash chemistry that breaks the bond between organic growth and substrate, then rinses with low-pressure pure water — leaving render visibly restored without the cracking risk of pressure washing.

  • K-Rend manufacturer-approved soft-wash methodology
  • Algae, lichen, moss and atmospheric staining all addressed
  • Optional 2–3 year biocide aftercare reduces regrowth
  • Suitable for new builds, period properties and apartment blocks
06 — Signage & rooftop branding

Your brand, sharp at every height.

Rooftop signage, illuminated totems, fascia branding, retail park monoliths — kept clean, photographed, and condition-reported in a single ground-based visit. Ideal for franchise estates, retail asset managers and outdoor media operators.

  • Multi-site rollout pricing for retail and franchise estates
  • Pre/post photography for marketing & landlord reporting
  • Condition reports flag broken bulbs, panel damage, mounting wear
  • Bird-deterrent reapplication available alongside cleaning
