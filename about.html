Cleaning, re-engineered.

We started Aerial Clean UK because the exterior cleaning industry was overdue a rethink. Scaffolding budgets, week-long projects, working-at-height fatalities — none of it had to keep happening. Drones changed the maths. We built a business around it.

Our story

A small, specialist team. Serious kit. National reach.

Aerial Clean UK is run from a base in Shirley, Solihull, by CAA-licensed pilots and trade-trained cleaning operatives. Every job — from a single domestic property to a multi-storey commercial estate — is run by a two-person crew: one pilot, one ground observer. Both fully insured, both fully briefed on the structure before a single rotor spins.

We invest in industrial-grade equipment built specifically for cleaning: heavy-lift platforms with dedicated pure-water reservoirs, calibrated soft-wash systems, thermal payloads for solar inspection, and 4K imaging on every flight. The result is a service that genuinely competes — on cost, on quality, and on speed — with the access methods we replace.

What we believe

Three principles. No exceptions.

The drone is a tool. The discipline behind it is what makes the difference.

Safety is non-negotiable

Operators stay grounded. RAMS issued before every job. No-fly zones logged with NATS where applicable. Two-person crew, every flight.

Communication beats discount

You'll know exactly when we're arriving, what we're doing, and what we've delivered. Same-day reports, no chasing for updates.

Evidence over assumption

Every job is documented. Pre-clean condition, methodology used, finished result — all captured and shared. No "trust us" service.
